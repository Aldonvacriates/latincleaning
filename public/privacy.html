Cookies & Similar Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to keep the site working smoothly, remember your preferences, and (if enabled) help us understand how the site is used. We group cookies into the categories below:

Examples are illustrative and may change as we update the site.
Category Purpose Examples
Essential Required for core functionality like remembering your cookie choice or keeping forms usable. lc_cookie_consent (remembers accept/decline), session state cookies.
Performance Helps us understand site traffic and usage to improve performance. Analytics cookies (only set if you accept cookies).
Functional Stores preferences (e.g., language) to personalize your experience. Preference cookies (only set if you accept cookies).
Advertising Used by advertising partners to show relevant ads. We do not use these by default. Third-party ad network cookies (not active on our site unless added in the future).

Your Choices About Cookies

  • Cookie banner: You can accept or decline non-essential cookies in the banner that appears on your first visit.
  • Reopen the banner: Use the button below to review or change your choice at any time.
  • Browser controls: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies in settings. This may affect some site features.

Managing Cookies in Your Browser

For convenience, here are general paths (they can vary by version):

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari (macOS): Settings → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
